About Us

Sunrise Rescue is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing hope and support to those in need. We believe that every life, human or animal, deserves a second chance. Our mission is to serve communities by providing essential resources, from food and clothing to safe havens for animals. Through our dedicated team of volunteers and the kindness of our donors, we are able to make a tangible difference in the lives of many, embodying the true spirit of compassion and rescue.

Our Work

Food Distribution

We organize regular food distribution drives to ensure that no one in our community goes hungry.

Clothes Donation

We collect and donate clothes to provide comfort and dignity to individuals and families in need.

Animal Rescue

Our team is committed to rescuing and rehabilitating stray and injured animals, giving them a chance at a loving home.
